Policies & House Rules

Please read all policies carefully before submitting a reservation inquiry.

Booking Terms

  • Submitting an inquiry does NOT guarantee a reservation. Booking is only confirmed after full payment of the required deposit and written confirmation from management.
  • Guests must be at least 18 years old to make a reservation.
  • All reservations are subject to availability.
  • Management reserves the right to refuse any reservation at their discretion.
  • The number of guests must not exceed the maximum occupancy stated for each unit (4 persons for Airbnb units).
  • Guests are required to present a valid government-issued ID upon check-in.
  • Check-in time is 2:00 PM. Check-out time is 12:00 PM (noon). Early check-in and late check-out may be accommodated subject to availability and additional fees.
🚫

Cancellation Policy

  • 7 or more days before check-in: Full refund of the reservation deposit, minus processing fees.
  • 3–6 days before check-in: 50% refund of the reservation deposit.
  • Less than 3 days before check-in: No refund. The deposit is forfeited.
  • Cancellations must be submitted in writing via email or Facebook message to management.
  • No-shows are treated as cancellations made less than 3 days before check-in — no refund.
  • In the event that JB's Apartment must cancel a confirmed reservation (due to force majeure or unforeseen circumstances), a full refund will be issued.
💰

Security Deposit Policy

  • A refundable security deposit is required to confirm all reservations.
  • Airbnb/Short-Term Units: Security deposit of ₱2,000.
  • Apartment/Long-Term Units: Two (2) months advance rent plus one (1) month security deposit is required upon move-in.
  • The security deposit is refunded within 7–14 business days after check-out, provided no damages or violations have occurred.
  • Deductions will be made for any damages, missing items, or unpaid utility bills before the deposit is refunded.
  • An itemized statement of deductions will be provided to the guest if any amount is withheld from the deposit.
🔧

Damages Policy

  • Guests are responsible for any damage to the unit, furniture, appliances, or fixtures caused during their stay.
  • Damage must be reported to management immediately upon discovery. Failure to report damages may result in additional charges.
  • Costs for repairs or replacements will be deducted from the security deposit. If damages exceed the deposit amount, the guest will be billed for the difference.
  • Normal wear and tear is expected and will not be charged to the guest.
  • Management reserves the right to document the condition of the unit before and after each stay through photos or inspection.
  • Deliberate or gross negligence damage may result in immediate termination of stay without refund, and legal action if warranted.
🏠

House Rules

  • No smoking inside the unit or common areas. Designated smoking areas are provided outside.
  • No pets allowed unless explicitly pre-approved in writing by management.
  • Quiet hours are from 10:00 PM to 7:00 AM. Please be considerate of other residents.
  • No parties or events inside the unit without prior written approval from management.
  • Visitors are allowed but must be registered at the front desk. Overnight visitors require management approval.
  • Guests must keep the unit and shared areas clean and tidy at all times.
  • Garbage must be properly disposed of in designated bins. Do not leave trash in hallways or common areas.
  • Tampering with CCTV cameras, fire alarms, or any building equipment is strictly prohibited.
  • Loud music or noise that disturbs neighbors will not be tolerated at any time.
  • Subletting the unit to a third party without management's written consent is strictly prohibited and grounds for immediate termination of tenancy without refund.
  • Management is not liable for any valuables left unattended inside the unit. Guests are advised to secure their belongings.
  • Violation of any house rule may result in immediate eviction without refund.
✅
By submitting a reservation inquiry, you confirm that you have read, understood, and agree to all the policies listed on this page. For clarifications, please message us on Facebook or send us an inquiry.
